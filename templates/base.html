About Us

Trotro.Live is the digital entity of your regular trotro transit. According to Wikipedia, in Ghana and neighboring countries trotros are private owned mini-buses or shared taxis that travel fixed routes leaving when filled to capacity. We provide technical support such as live update on the original fare, routing of buses, recovering of missing items and others. TrotroLive, Dream in Color.
